FATHEIR'S DAY Prince William beams as he plays with George, Charlotte and Louis in joyous snaps celebrating Father’s Day

DDaniel Hammond 20 Jun 2020, 22:35 Updated: 20 Jun 2020, 22:52



PROUD dad Prince William and his three children beam in two snaps released by the Duchess of Cambridge to mark Father’s Day.

Photographer Kate took a picture of George, six, Charlotte, five, and Louis, two, on a swing with William — who also celebrates his 38th birthday tomorrow.


George, Charlotte and Louis posed on a swing with Prince William in a heart-warming snap
The family rolled around on the grass in a touching photo released by Kate Middleton to mark Father’s Day

In another heart-warming snap, the children clamber over the Duke’s back in the garden at their Norfolk home.

And the bond between the family is clear, with Princess Charlotte draped around William’s neck to show she is daddy’s girl.

Meanwhile a mischievous Prince George hung from the swing as Louis showed off his smile sitting on the Duke’s lap.

The handmade swing is engraved with “William and Catherine” and was a gift for their 2011 wedding.

It was created by family business The Oak & Rope Company.

The Kent firm sells similar items today for £455.


Kate recently launched her Hold Still photography project, where she will curate 100 images taken during the pandemic for an exhibitionCredit: Getty - Contributor

Snapper Kate even captured her family rolling around on the grass in a rare snapshot of family life in lockdown.

Kate, 38, recently launched her Hold Still photography project, where she will curate 100 images taken during the pandemic for an exhibition.

She first proved her talent after releasing official photos of Princess Charlotte in June 2015.
